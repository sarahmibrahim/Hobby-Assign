Knitting for Newbies

Welcome to my knitting page here you will learn how to make a simple neck warmer and or a hat using the same pattern



Below is a YouTube video showing how to make the red hat. You will need chunky yarn and a round knitting needle 10mm in size. You will also need a stitch maker. Depending on the size of hat you will need to cast on 16 stitches (always an even number)

To make the neck warmer you would follow the video untill you have knitted 10 rows and stop then finish the neck warmer by casting off (see video below for casting off your neck warmer).



To learn how to cast off your neck warmer if you do not want to make hat please watch the video below
